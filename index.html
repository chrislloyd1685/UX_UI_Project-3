Questions & Answers

 How can ExRemedy help me?

ExRemedy is designed to checkin with you daily to see how you feel. We know that recovering from a breakup isn't easy and that some days are worse than others. By checking in, ExRemedy can recommend curated content for you that will support you in your journey. Whether you need to be distracted or whether you want to educate yourself on healthy relationships.

Why do I need to learn about relationships?

Being in a relationship, whether platonic or romantic, is one of the most important parts of being a human. Our connections help shape us. So learning about relationships is a way to make sure that those connections stay healthy.

How do I use the journal feature?

To use the journal feature, click the journal icon on the tab bar. There you will be able to choose between creating a new entry, viewing or editing past entries, or deleting them. Remember this is your space to share or save what you want!

Do I need to use ExRemedy daily?

We recommend using ExRemedy daily for optimal recovery time, but we understand everyone has different schedules! Even using our app weekly can have some benefits. Getting over a breakup and moving forward is hard, so be kind to yourself and use ExRemedy when you need to.
